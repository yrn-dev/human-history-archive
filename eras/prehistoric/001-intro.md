# Prehistoric Era: Introduction

## Overview

The prehistoric era spans from the emergence of Homo sapiens (~300,000 years ago) to the invention of writing (~3000 BCE).

## Key Periods

| Period | Dates | Key Developments |
|--------|-------|------------------|
| Paleolithic | 300,000-10,000 BCE | Stone tools, fire, language |
| Neolithic | 10,000-3,000 BCE | Agriculture, pottery, settlements |
| Bronze Age | 3,000-1,200 BCE | Metalworking, cities, writing |

## Major Discoveries

- **Fire** (~1,000,000 BCE)
- **Stone tools** (~300,000 BCE)
- **Language** (~100,000 BCE)
- **Agriculture** (~10,000 BCE)
- **Writing** (~3,000 BCE)
